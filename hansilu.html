SPACE AGE

The game that teaches cybersecurity in the most enjoyably grinding setting yet, instructing players to combat digital threats with real world solutions.

FAQ Back to games

Overview

This game teaches cybersafety for children in an enjoyable, yet challenging format, guiding them to the right solution for each problem in a fun and enjoyable manner. By putting different statistics and counters for each threat and their solution, it guides players to the proper resolution for the threat involved. The tower defense aspect will be updated in SPACE AGE (v2), with a completely new defense and battle mechanic.
