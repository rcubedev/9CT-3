Digital Crisis Simulator

Race against the clock in a faithful 1990s desktop: pop‑ups, and deceptive dialogs flood the screen. Stop the threats before they execute - act fast and choose carefully to avoid a total system meltdown.

FAQ Back to games

Overview

Threats appear as windows or dialogs. Some mimic legitimate programs and require careful judgement. Make sure to quickly block threats before they "execute". The longer you take, the more your system instability increases; let it reach the limit and the game ends with a BSOD.

Educational Content

This game teaches players to identify and respond to common cybersecurity threats through interactive challenges. By simulating viruses, phishing emails, and malware in a retro desktop environment, players learn to spot red flags, understand malware types, and practice safe digital behaviour.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I make the game run better?

Make sure to use TurboWarp, and enable Turbo mode and 60 FPS mode! You can do this by holding ⇧ Shift + ⎇ Alt (⇧ Shift + ⌥ Option on Mac) when clicking the green flag. This will ensure the game runs as smoothly as possible. Also try closing other tabs/programs, and enabling hardware acceleration in your browser.

What are the controls?

Actions are mouse-driven - use the mouse to identify the threats.
