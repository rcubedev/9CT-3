Overview

This game teaches cybersafety for children in an enjoyable, yet challenging format, guiding them to the right solution for each problem in a fun and enjoyable manner. By putting different statistics and counters for each threat and their solution, it guides players to the proper resolution for the threat involved. The tower defense aspect will be updated in SPACE AGE (v2), with a completely new defense and battle mechanic.

Frequently Asked Questions

What age is Space Age suitable for?

Space Age is designed for players aged 8+. Levels and language are classroom-friendly; teachers can enable guided mode for younger groups.

Do I need an account to play?

No account is required for free play. Creating a teacher or parent account unlocks progress tracking and classroom features.

Can I use this in a classroom setting?

Yes â€” there's a teacher mode with discussion prompts and session controls. See the teacher guide in the repo or contact us for curriculum packs.

How is student data handled?

We prioritise privacy. By default no personal data is uploaded. Teacher accounts explicitly opt in for anonymised progress reporting.

Will my progress be saved between sessions?

Progress is saved locally in the browser for anonymous play. Teacher accounts can save across devices via the cloud sync option.

Where can I report bugs or request features?

Please open an issue on the project repository or email support (see Contact in the footer). Include device, browser and a short description of the problem.
